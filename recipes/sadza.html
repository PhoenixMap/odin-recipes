Sadza Recipe

Description

The process of cooking Zimbabwe's most popular staple food

Ingredients

  • Mealie-meal
  • Water

Steps

  1. Boil the water in a pot
  2. Create a paste using the mealie-meal in a separate bowl
  3. Pour the paste in the boiling water while mixing gently with a wooden spoon
  4. Let it simmer and boil for about 7 minutes
  5. Add more mealie-meal bit by bit as you mix it into a thicker paste
  6. Let it simmer at low heat after achieving your prefered thickness
  7. Yeah! It's ready to serve.
