Usavi Recipe

Description

The process of cooking Zimbabwe's most popular relish to go with the staple food.

Ingredients

  • Covo or Rape vegetables
  • Cooking oil
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps

  1. Cut the vegetables into thin strips
  2. Put cooking oil in a frying pan and heat it
  3. Cut the onion and tomatoes
  4. Add the vegetable strips into the pan
  5. Fry as you add a little water
  6. Add onion, tomatoes and salt
  7. Yeah! It's ready to serve.
