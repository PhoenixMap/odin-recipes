Maheu Recipe

Description

The process of brewing Zimbabwe's most popular traditional drink

Ingredients

  • Stale sadza
  • Chimera
  • Water

Steps

  1. Crush the stale sadza in a kalabash
  2. Add water
  3. Mix in the chimera
  4. Leave it covered and set for about 3 to 4 days
  5. Yeah! It's ready to serve. Add sugar if need be.
